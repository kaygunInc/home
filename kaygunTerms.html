2. Auto-Renewal Terms (Core Terms)

To ensure uninterrupted service, this product adopts an auto-renewal model:

  • Account Payment: Upon confirmation of purchase, the payment will be charged to your iTunes account associated with your Apple ID.
  • Auto-Renewal Mechanism: Unless you manually cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period, the subscription will automatically renew for the original cycle, and the Apple account will be charged within 24 hours prior to expiration.
  • Renewal Price: Unless we notify you of a price adjustment in advance, the renewal price is usually the same as the standard price at the time of the initial purchase.

  • Cancellation of Trial: If you do not wish to be charged after the trial period ends, please ensure you manually cancel the subscription at least 24 hours before the trial ends.
  • Consumption of Trial Benefits: If you purchase a paid subscription during the free trial period (if offered), any unused portion of the free trial time will be forfeited.

4. Subscription Management and Cancellation

You have full control over your subscriptions. To cancel the auto-renewal, please follow these steps:

  • Open your iOS device "Settings".
  • Tap your [Apple ID/Name] at the top.
  • Go to "Subscriptions".
  • Find and tap this product in the list.
  • Tap "Cancel Subscription" at the bottom of the page.

Note: Uninstalling the App does not automatically cancel the subscription. You must manually stop the renewal through the system settings mentioned above.
